A File Format for Kitchen Recipes

2020-10-27T13:43:14-07:00

Those of us who cook will know the frustration of having to scroll to the end of a long food-blog post to get to the recipe. Additionally frustrating is the lack of a good way to save these recipes, forcing us to bookmark these pages and go through the scrolling process again and again. Promoting a standardized file format for these recipes would alleviate this problem, and make it easier to maintain a digital personal recipe book.
